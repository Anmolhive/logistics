Mission

The "Landmarks in Logistics Summit’ aims to innovate, indicate, and improve global logistics efficiency and sustainability. The summit will embrace an array of global logistics and supply chain experts to discuss the latest trends, techniques, research, and innovations in the industry. The open forum is to bring together global logistics pioneers, practitioners, policymakers, government entities, and private organizations under a common roof to address the key challenges, review the available solutions with a regional focus, and engage in advocacy.

Landmarks In Logistics KSA Summit 2023

Logistics and supply chain management in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is one of the key elements of the Vision 2030, as Saudi is striving to position itself as the logistics hub of the region. This contributes toward supporting the logistics and supply chains of the country and around the world. A diligent summit focused on the sector is very much required to stay abreast of the advancements being made in the logistics and supply chain sectors.

The "Landmarks in Logistics Summit’ shall discuss the key challenges affecting the steady headway in the region. It will provide the platform to analyze how smart logistics is used in many areas to apply optimal logistic structure management and to make the best traffic flow decisions based on the available data and how recent technologies such as the IOT, big data analytics, smart transportation, and other cutting-edge technologies can

play an important role in discovering optimal logistics solutions.
Summit will also put digitization, green logistics, and e-procurements in the focal point using various presentation formats, including keynote addresses by the eminent speakers, panel discussions, and question-and-answer sessions, to ensure the highest levels of interaction among the invited global participants.
